Expressive TTS Poster

This poster explores cutting-edge advancements in expressive Text-to-Speech (TTS) systems, focusing on style representation, multilingual adaptability, and applications of expressive speech synthesis.

Details About the Poster

The poster highlights key advancements in expressive TTS, including:

  • Techniques for style integration, such as SALN and semantic tokens, enabling precise control over prosody and timbre.
  • Leveraging text foundation models for capturing complex semantics and varied speaking styles.
  • Applications of expressive TTS in modeling conversational and emotional speech with enhanced datasets.

Images and figures in the poster illustrate these methods and their applications in creating natural and adaptive speech synthesis.
