Expressive TTS Poster

This poster explores cutting-edge advancements in expressive Text-to-Speech (TTS) systems, focusing on style representation, multilingual adaptability, datasets, and applications of expressive speech synthesis.

Details About the Poster

The poster highlights advancements in expressive TTS, focusing on:

  • **Style Integration:** AS-Speech and CosyVoice introduce techniques for fine-grained style integration, such as SALN and supervised semantic tokens, enabling precise control over prosody, timbre, and rhythm.
  • **Text Foundation Models:** FireRedTTS leverages text foundation models to capture complex semantics and varied speaking styles, providing robust solutions for expressive and adaptive TTS.
  • **Datasets for Expressiveness:** StoryTTS provides a richly annotated dataset for text and acoustic alignment, enhancing the ability to capture diverse speech styles and emotional content.
  • **Style Representations:** UniStyle introduces UniConnector for aligning speaking styles across text and speech modalities, enabling better stylistic consistency and synthesis.
  • **Prosody and Timbre Control:** ControlSpeech and studies combining emotional speech datasets demonstrate advanced controllability over prosody, timbre, and style using natural language prompts.
  • **Applications:** Expressive TTS systems are applied in conversational AI, multilingual speech synthesis, and generating speech that adapts dynamically to user inputs and contextual requirements.

Images and figures in the poster illustrate these methods and their applications in creating natural, adaptable, and context-aware speech synthesis systems.
